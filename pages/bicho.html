Cada carta de Bug le dará al Axie 3 Moral y 1 HP adicionales . Jugar cartas de clase Bug dará una bonificación de daño adicional del 10%, pero no estás limitado a usar solo cartas de clase acuática. La clase Bug casi nunca se ve en una configuración de equipo Axie. La mayoría de sus tarjetas obtienen una puntuación baja en la lista de niveles de tarjetas. La clase Bug tiene muchas cartas que aplican Debuffs al usarlas..

Allergic Reaction: Deal 130% damage to debuffed targets.

Anesthetic Bait: Apply stun when struck by Aquatic or Bird class cards. Can only trigger once per round.

Barb Strike: Apply poison to target when played in chain.


Blood Taste: Heal this Axie by the damage inflicted with this card.

Bug Noise: Apply Attack- to target.

Bug Signal:Steal energy from your opponent when chained with another "Bug Signal" card.


Bug Splat:Deal 50% more damage when attacking Bug targets.

Buzzing Wind: Apply Fragile to target until next round.

Chemical Warfare: Apply Stentch to enemy for 1 round.


Disguise: Gain 1 energy when comboed with a plant card.

Dull Grip: Deal 30% more damage to shielded targets.

Grub Explode: Deal 200% damage when attacking in Last Stand. Axie's Last Stand ends after it attacks.


Grub Surprise: Apply Fear to shielded targets

Mite Bite: Add 100% more damage when comboed with another card.

Mystic Rush: Apply Speed- to target for 2 rounds.


Numbing Lecretion: Disable target's melee cards next round.

Sticky Goo: Stun attacker if this Axie's shield breaks. Can only trigger once per round.

Sunder Claw: Randomly discard 1 card from your enemy's hand.

Terror Chomp: Apply Fear to target for 2 turns when played in a Chain.

Third Glance: Randomly discard 1 card from your enemy's hand.

Twin Needle: Attack twice when comboed with another card.


Scarab Curse: Target cannot be healed for 2 rounds.
